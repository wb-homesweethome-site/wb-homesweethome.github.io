Now Casting - Home Sweet Home

HOME SWEET HOME is a fun one-hour unscripted family social experiment that invites participants to explore living environments different from their own and learn lessons that come only when we truly walk a mile in another’s shoes. Two families from wildly different backgrounds will swap houses for one week to immerse themselves in how the other side lives. Each family must eat the food, engage in the hobbies and interact with the neighbors of the family with which they’ve traded homes. By the end of each episode at a huge joint family dinner, the participants will have learned more about themselves as a unit and as individuals in context to the wider world. This social experiment will delve into the cultural differences that exist - both big and small - and ask if the grass really is greener on the other side. Will the families yearn for home sweet home or find they have more in common with others than they thought?
